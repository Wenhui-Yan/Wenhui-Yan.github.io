A Subtractive Content Strategy

2026-02-10 · Methodology

I deliberately moved away from broad and scattered writing and focused on topics that can compound over time. This post records three adjustments in topic selection, structure, and publishing rhythm.

Topic Selection: Fewer, but Deeper

I first list everything I want to write, then rank topics by reusability and long-term value, and turn the top 20% into ongoing series.

Structure: One-Page Density

Each article contains a conclusion, process, and action checklist, so readers can take away the core idea within five minutes.

Publishing: Fixed Cadence

I publish at a fixed weekly time to avoid being driven only by bursts of inspiration.
